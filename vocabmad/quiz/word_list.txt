Mnemonic trick 1: Apple के बढ़ते price Appall करते हैं ☺


Word: Apparent
Pronunciation: (अपैरन्ट)(əˈpær ənt)(adj.) 
Hindi meaning: स्पष्ट, साफ़-साफ ; अवास्तविक, मिथ्या 
EM 1: clearly visible or understood; obvious EM 1 EX 1: His opponent’s superiority was apparent (clear) in the first half of the game. EM 1 EX 2: The truth behind your story is now apparent to me. (I can clearly see the truth) EM 2: seeming to be real or true, but not necessarily so EM 2 EX 1: The victim died of an apparent heart attack. (it appears that a heart attack caused his death) Synonym: Syn: evident, obvious, transparent, discernible, conspicuous, seeming, superficial, supposed
Mnemonic trick 1:Apparent = transparent = साफ़-साफ़ और स्पष्ट दिखने वाला  Mnemonic trick 2:ऐसा लगा मुझे कि वो ‘अ’ (बिना) parent है, मतलब बिना माँ बाप का है, लेकिन जैसा कि मैंने कहा कि ऐसा लगा केवल, ऐसा था नहीं so he was an apparent orphan (मतलब असली में अ parent या अनाथ नहीं था)
